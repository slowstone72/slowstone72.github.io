"kmart" - Video by Callum Fisher

"kmart" is a short edit of an old Kmart commercial. I edited the audio in Audacity. The video was originally edited in LoiLoScope, but I re-edited it in Shotcut on the 13th of December 2022 to hide the LoiLoScope watermark. Removal of the watermark was achieved by changing the aspect ratio to 4:3 so that the watermark was out of view. The initial version of the video was longer and featured a compilation of Kmart bankruptcy news clips, and similar things. Date of creation: 13.12.2022
