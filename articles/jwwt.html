"just when we thought" - Video by Callum Fisher

"just when we thought" is a video featuring looped segments of Prefab Sprout's "The Sound of Crying". The visuals were recorded in Garry's Mod on the map Gm_York_Remaster_Night.

The video was edited in Shotcut and Microsoft Photos Video Editor. The audio was edited in Audacity.
