Devotion

It's very easy
to lose your grip
To wind up
boarding a sinking ship
And we've all been there
admit it or don't
Well, if you're weak
I know you won't

Some paths we travel
may wave goodbye
In the blink
of a straining eye

When you're drowning
in a crowded ocean
When you're losing
all of your devotion
I'll still be there for you
ready or not
And I will pull you out
and hold you
I've already told you
